What we do

Three pillars, one mission.

Malaria is a solvable problem. We attack it from three sides — generating evidence, mobilizing communities, and supporting the systems that already work.

01 / Research

Data-driven evidence

Household surveys, drug shop assessments, GPS-tagged hazard mapping. We collect the granular data that policymakers and NGOs need but rarely have access to.

02 / Advocacy

Community mobilization

Awareness campaigns, school outreach, household sensitization. We meet people where they are and turn knowledge into behavior change.

03 / Intervention

On-the-ground action

Net distribution, drainage clearance, drug shop partnerships, and IRS coordination. Real interventions, tracked and reported transparently.
