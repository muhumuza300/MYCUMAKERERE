Part 07 · About the Field Study

The team behind the data.

This project was carried out by students from Makerere University in collaboration with the College of Engineering, Design, Art & Technology. Trained enumerators conducted the surveys over two weeks in April 2026, with all data captured via Google Forms.

Partnership & Acknowledgments

This field study was conducted in collaboration with students from Makerere University's College of Engineering, Design, Art & Technology, bringing together expertise in public health, data science, GIS mapping, and community engagement.

Special thanks to the residents of Kasanvu, local community leaders, Village Health Teams, and the Kisugu Health Centre III staff for their cooperation and support throughout this study.
