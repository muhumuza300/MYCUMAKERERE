Partnership Opportunities

Let's work together.

We're always open to partnerships that strengthen our impact. Here are the areas where we're actively seeking collaboration.

NGOs & Health Orgs

Working in Kampala or Uganda's informal settlements? Let's coordinate so we don't duplicate efforts and can share data, methodologies, and field teams.

Researchers

Our datasets are open for academic collaboration. We welcome partnerships on epidemiology, GIS analysis, behavioral health, and intervention science.

Corporate Sponsors

Looking for high-impact CSR partnerships? We offer transparent, evidence-driven projects with clear deliverables and reporting.

Student Volunteers

From any university, any discipline. We need data analysts, field enumerators, designers, writers, and community organizers.

Health Facilities

Local clinics, drug shops, and Village Health Teams — your case data and frontline insights shape everything we do. Let's build that partnership.

Media & Journalists

We make our data, photos, and team available for stories that bring attention to malaria and the communities we work with.
